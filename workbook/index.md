## Free Workbook: Questions & Checklist

Whether you're about to meet your first web agency or you're halfway through
a project that's already going sideways, this workbook pulls together every
question from *What to Ask Your Website Company* into a single document you
can print out and take with you.

For each chapter — from setting your goals to spotting red flags — there are
questions to ask yourself and questions to put to your developer, with space
to write down their answers. At the end, there's a full checklist to tick
off before you sign anything.
